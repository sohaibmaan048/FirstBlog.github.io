## 1. A Year of Challenges and Growth

Pichla saal mushkilat se bhara tha lekin usne mujhe bohat kuch sikhaya. Meri journey ke teen bare pehlu ye hain:

* **Facing ECAT with Limited Preparation**
* **Overcoming Academic Setbacks**
* **New Life in Faisalabad Hostel**

### The Struggle and Success
Last year was full of challenges, especially preparing for **ECAT** with very limited time. At first, I felt completely overwhelmed and unsure if I could manage the exam. I had to focus on the most important topics, prioritize practice over theory, and push myself every day. 

![Desktop View](/assets/img/img.jpg)

Slowly, I began to gain confidence as I solved past papers and reviewed key concepts regularly. Alongside the exam preparation, I also faced academic setbacks in some subjects. It was hard to accept failure at first, but I learned that setbacks are part of the learning process. 

> "By analyzing my mistakes, asking for help when needed, and improving my study methods, I gradually started to recover."

### Hostel Life in Faisalabad
Living in the **Faisalabad hostel** for the first time added another layer of challenge. Managing my own meals, chores, and daily schedule was unfamiliar and difficult initially. 

However, I adapted over time, learned independence, and made friends from different backgrounds. These experiences taught me:
1. **Resilience and Patience**
2. **Responsibility and Teamwork**
3. **Effective Communication**

The hostel experience shaped both my academic and personal growth, making me more independent than ever before.
