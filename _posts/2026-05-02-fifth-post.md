## Database Work: Normalization and Lab Practice 📊

I continued my focus on Database concepts, deep-diving into **Normalization**. I covered both **First Normal Form (1NF)** and **Second Normal Form (2NF)**, moving from theory to practical lab work on both.

Practicing these concepts was essential; it helped me understand exactly how to structure data properly and systematic ways to remove data redundancy. The benefit of organized tables in robust database design is now much clearer.

Alongside my database work, I started researching a suitable dataset for an upcoming Machine Learning task, which provided an interesting parallel learning experience.

However, the week included a distinct disciplinary lesson. My instructor deducted marks during lab work because I was using a mobile device. The expectation was to maintain consistency and a professional working approach in the lab.

> **Key Takeaway**
